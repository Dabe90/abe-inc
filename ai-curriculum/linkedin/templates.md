# LinkedIn & public progress templates

**Cadence:** 4 posts / month (see canvas). Teach what you just struggled with.

---

## Monthly pattern

| Week | Type | Goal |
|------|------|------|
| 1 | Learn | One concept, one diagram |
| 2 | Build | Screenshot / repo / curve |
| 3 | Operator | How this helps churches/ops/SMBs |
| 4 | Milestone | Ship link + ask for critique |

---

## Template A — Concept

```text
I used to find [TOPIC] confusing.

Plain English:
[2–4 sentences]

Diagram / sketch: [attach]

Why it matters for real products:
[1–2 sentences]

Week [N] of my public AI curriculum.
```

---

## Template B — Build in public

```text
Shipped: [P# name]

Stack: [tools]
Result: [metric]
Cost/time: [$ / hours]

What broke:
1. ...
2. ...

Repo: [link]

Feedback welcome — especially on [eval / UX / safety].
```

---

## Template C — Operator translation

```text
Operators don’t need bigger models first.

They need:
→ clear evals
→ human approval on side effects
→ ownership of data/code

Here’s how [thing I learned] shows up in volunteer / enrollment / publishing workflows…
```

---

## Template D — Honesty / anti-hype

```text
I’m not training a trillion-parameter model in my bedroom.

I am building the skills to:
- fine-tune & evaluate honestly
- ship governed agents
- eventually lead a lab/team

Curriculum notes (open): [link to ai-curriculum README]
```
