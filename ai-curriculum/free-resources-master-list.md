# Free materials master list

All links below are **free to access** (courseware, PDFs, videos, or open weights). Paid certificates are optional — you don’t need them.

**How to use:** When a year-note says “see master list,” come here. Prefer **one primary path** per topic so you don’t drown.

## Suggested “do not buy yet” list

Skip paid bootcamps early. Free > enough for Years 0–2.  
Spend money later on: **GPU hours**, maybe one conference, lawyer when you incorporate.

**Year 0:** 3Blue1Brown + ISL + MIT 6.036 + NumPy  
**Year 1:** Karpathy + HF Course + CS224n + D2L/fast.ai  
**Year 2:** vLLM + PEFT/Axolotl + agent papers + your Abe Stack agents  
**Year 3:** arXiv weekly + reproduce one idea  
**Year 4–5:** YC Startup School + real customers + safety policy
