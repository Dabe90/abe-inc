Prayer City Social

Multi-platform social publishing platform — compose once, generate AI-tailored variants, schedule or publish to 10 networks from one dashboard.

social.prayercityhtx.com ↗

Problem

Posting the same content to every platform by hand is slow and repetitive. Subscription schedulers are expensive, rented, and limited in what they connect to.

Solution

  • Next.js dashboard with one compose box and AI variants per network
  • 10 platforms — Instagram, Facebook, YouTube, TikTok, LinkedIn, X, Threads, Bluesky, Spotify, WhatsApp
  • Queue-based worker for scheduled and instant publishing with retry logic
  • OAuth account linking with token refresh for each provider
  • In-browser video editor for Reels and short-form clips
  • Multi-provider AI layer (Groq, DeepSeek, OpenAI) with automatic failover
  • PocketBase auth, media storage, and job records behind Cloudflare Tunnel
