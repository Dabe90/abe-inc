Problem

Academy inquiries arrived as unstructured form data and PDF attachments. Staff manually triaged submissions, chased missing fields, and wrote follow-up emails by hand — slowing response time and losing context between threads.

Solution

  • Tool-calling agent classifies each submission: complete, incomplete, or needs clarification
  • Flags missing enrollment fields with specific next-step prompts for staff
  • Drafts personalized follow-up emails in the academy's voice for human review
  • Logs every inquiry to Google Sheets for reporting and audit trail
  • Sends approved replies via Gmail API — no unsupervised outbound mail

Results

Enrollment moved from PDF email threads to guided wizard plus intelligent follow-up. Helena's team replies faster with full context on every inquiry. Triage time dropped an estimated 85%; every submission is logged and routed — zero lost attachments.
