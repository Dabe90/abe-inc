Royal Wealth Heritage LLC

Custom business website and academy enrollment platform for a Texas financial consulting firm and its RWH Life Skills & Learning Academy division.

royalwealthheritage.com ↗

Problem

Royal Wealth Heritage needed a professional web presence for two divisions — financial consulting and an educational academy — plus a way to collect enrollment paperwork without email attachments or paper forms.

Solution

  • 11-page Next.js site with custom branding (magenta, gold, cream palette)
  • 7-step academy enrollment wizard with 7 standalone legal forms
  • Contact and enrollment forms delivered to info@royalwealthheritage.com via Web3Forms
  • SEO assets — sitemap, Open Graph images, page metadata
  • Zoho Mail setup with MX, SPF, and DKIM on Vercel DNS
  • Mobile-first UX with accessible navigation and print-friendly forms
