Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 3, 2026

Abe Stack (“we,” “us”) operates abestack.com. This policy explains what information we collect when you use our website and how we use it.

Information we collect

  • Contact forms — name, email, and message content when you submit an inquiry or download our checklist.
  • Scheduling — name and email when you book a call through Calendly.
  • Usage data — standard server and analytics logs (pages visited, browser type, approximate location) if analytics tools are enabled.

How we use information

We use your information to respond to inquiries, deliver requested resources, schedule meetings, improve our website, and provide services you hire us for. We do not sell your personal information.

Third-party services

We use trusted providers to operate the site, including:

  • Vercel — hosting
  • FormSubmit — form delivery to our inbox
  • Calendly — appointment scheduling
  • Zoho Mail — business email
  • Google Search Console — search performance (aggregate data)

Each provider has its own privacy policy governing how they handle data.

Cookies

Our site may use essential cookies and similar technologies required for basic functionality. Third-party embeds (such as Calendly) may set their own cookies when you interact with them.

Data retention

We retain contact and project-related information as long as needed to respond to you, fulfill services, or comply with legal obligations.

Your rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of personal information we hold about you by emailing hello@abestack.com.

Children

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top reflects the latest revision.
