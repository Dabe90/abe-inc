Pricing FAQ

How is AI agent pricing determined?

Scope drives price: number of agents, tool integrations (Gmail, Sheets, Firestore, external APIs), RAG corpus size, review UI complexity, and timeline. Most add-ons land $4K–$9K; multi-agent platforms with custom dashboards reach $12K+. See the AI service page or book a discovery call for a fixed quote.

Do you offer payment plans?

Typically 50% to start, 50% at launch. Larger projects can be split across milestones.

What's included in every project?

Source code in your GitHub repo, production deploy, handoff docs, and post-launch support window. See our launch checklist.
